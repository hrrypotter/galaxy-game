Invisible Commands

Commands work in groups but are hidden from the bot list:

  • • @all - Mentions every group member.
  • • @admin - Mentions only group admins.
  • • /cancel - Stops the tagging process.
  • • Stop Button: Click "Stop" in chat to end process instantly.
